More People - Branding & Website design/build

MorePeople were after a modern, responsive website which could enhance their exisitng branding. They wanted the ability to be able to post their agency jobs onto the website via an admin system. They also has an already exisitng blog which we integrated with to bring into the newly designed website.

I created them some homepage concepts using Illustrator, which I felt were more inline with their branding and direction. After a few rounds of feedback the designs were ready to be built into working HTML pages. I followed my general practice by using Hammer to compile my use of my SASS, LESS and bootstrap source.

After making the needed adjustments to ensure the site is 100% cross-browser compliant and device responsive, I passed over the compiled code to the development team to complete features throughout the site before launching.
