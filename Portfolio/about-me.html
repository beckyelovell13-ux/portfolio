About me.
find out more about me...

I love interior design as well as the obvious digital design! I've recently bought my first home with my partner and am enjoying renovating when possible.

My main passion in life is dogs. I have a miniature goldendoodle named Willow, she even has her own instagram account. I organise a weekly dog meet up in my village, and really enjoy training, learning about dog nutrition and watching wildlife documentaries.

Understanding more about human & animal psychology really interests me. I'm currently taking a course on The Psycology of interaction design in my free time.

I care greatly about the wellbeing of people. So much so that I am a member of the 'Wellbeings' team at TIDE. This involves being part of a small team who meet bi-weekly to discuss new ideas around content/events which can be arranged in the workplace to ensure we are looking after all employees.

I am a proud plant mum to a; Palmour Palm, a Boston Fern & a Ceropegia woodii.
